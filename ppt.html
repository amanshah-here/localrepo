Team EmpowerHer

A New Standard for Digital Safety

The Problem: A Silent Struggle

  • Vulnerability: Young girls lack the tools to recognize and report online abuse, leaving them unprotected.
  • Fear of Reporting: Many incidents go unreported due to fear, embarrassment, or not knowing who to turn to.
  • Ineffective Solutions: Current safety programs are often reactive and fail to address the complex psychological tactics used by abusers.
  • Parental Gap: Parents often feel unprepared to have difficult conversations about digital safety and exploitation.

Our Solution: A Proactive Companion

Introducing SafeHer, the all-in-one app that empowers girls with knowledge, skills, and a safe channel for help.

AI Safety Guardian

Our AI model analyzes conversations to proactively identify and flag subtle manipulative language.

Practical Self-Defense

Offers animated, easy-to-learn self-defense lessons, accessible anywhere, even offline.

Secure & Anonymous Reporting

A one-tap SOS and a private, evidence-gathering portal for reporting incidents with confidence.

Why SafeHer Is Different

Proactive Prevention

Our AI doesn't just react; it teaches users to spot red flags and avoid danger before it escalates.

Holistic Safety

We address both physical and emotional threats, providing a comprehensive safety solution.

Seamless Reporting

We offer an intuitive, secure pathway to report incidents, eliminating barriers to seeking help.

Designed for Trust

The app's anonymous and gamified design builds trust and makes safety education engaging for all ages.
