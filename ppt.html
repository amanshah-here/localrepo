Team EmpowerHer

A Digital Safety & Awareness Companion

The Problem

  • Early Vulnerability: Children as young as 5-10 lack awareness of safety rules and emotional boundaries.
  • Parental Hesitation: Parents struggle to discuss sensitive topics like abuse and exploitation.
  • Knowledge Gaps: Girls may not recognize inappropriate behavior or manipulation and fear reporting it.
  • Limited Solutions: Existing programs rarely cover both physical and emotional safety, leaving a critical gap.

Our Solution: SafeHer App

A digital safety and awareness companion focused on three key pillars:

AI Conversation Scanner

Detects “red flag” language and educates users on manipulative patterns.

Self-Defense Training

Provides short, animated micro-lessons on basic self-defense techniques.

Safe Reporting & Support

Features an SOS button and an anonymous portal for reporting incidents securely.

Key Features

AI Safety Coach

Detects manipulative chat tones and provides real-time feedback.

Self-Defense Library

Simple, visual, and available offline for maximum accessibility.

Secure Report Portal

Allows for secure, anonymous evidence collection for authorities.

Gamified Learning

Rewards users for completing lessons, making safety education engaging.

Benefits & Impact

Prevention

Teaches girls to identify manipulative behavior before it escalates into a dangerous situation.

Empowerment

Provides practical skills and confidence through self-defense training and knowledge.

Action

Offers a safe, digital evidence-collection and reporting mechanism.

Accessibility

The app is designed to be accessible to a wide audience, including offline functionality.

Why Judges Will Like It

  • Critical Social Issue: The app directly tackles a major problem in society – women's safety.
  • Innovative Approach: It uniquely combines AI, education, and civic utility into one cohesive platform.
  • Practical & Impactful: This isn't just a theoretical solution; it's a practical tool that can be used by millions of women and girls.
  • Unique from Competitors: It stands out from typical educational apps by focusing on prevention and providing a tangible reporting system.
